# Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 2026

> PUSHER does not collect your data. Everything stays on your device.

## Overview
PUSHER is built with privacy as a foundation. All data you enter — workouts, health metrics, medications, supplements, body measurements, sleep logs — is stored locally on your device and, if you choose, synced through your private iCloud account. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

## Data we collect
We do not collect any data. PUSHER does not include analytics SDKs, crash-reporting services, or advertising frameworks. No information leaves your device except through your own iCloud account under Apple's privacy protections.

## Apple Health integration
With your permission, the app reads steps, sleep, resting heart rate, HRV, and body weight from Apple Health, and can write workouts and body weight back. This data never leaves your device or iCloud account. You can revoke Health permissions at any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices.

## iCloud sync
If iCloud is enabled, your data is encrypted and synced across your own devices via Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. Only you can access this data — the developers have no access to it.

## No third parties
We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your data to any third party. The app contains no advertising, no tracking pixels, and no third-party SDKs that collect user data.

## Data deletion
You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app. To remove iCloud data, go to iOS Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage and delete the PUSHER backup.
